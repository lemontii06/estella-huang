About Me

Estella is an undergraduate at New York University, majoring in Digital Applications of Arts and Design with a minor in the Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology. She is passionate about the commercialization of art and design, particularly through technical integrations and marketing that drives business innovation.

As an interdisciplinary artist, photographer, and web developer, Estella seeks to tell compelling stories through her work, often exploring the aesthetics of life and the visual impacts.
